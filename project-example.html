Landscaper

This project is a game created for General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive Course. You play as a landscaper who cuts grass every day. You start off making a dollar a day by cutting grass with your teeth, but when you have enough money, you can go to the hardware store and upgrade your tool to something fancier. You keep cutting grass and buying new tools until you win the game by having the team of starving students and $1000 in the bank.
