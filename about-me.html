About Me

Hi, my name is Sara Meixner! I'm a 3D Artist and soon-to-be Software Engineer. I graduated from Drexel University with a B.S. in Animation in Visual Effects and a minor in Computer Science, and I am currently enrolled in Genreal Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive Course. My passion lies where art and creativity intersect with technology.

I grew up in northern New Jersey, but I am currently living in Philadelphia. Most of my time is spent learning how to code, but in my limited free time, I enjoy reading science fiction novels, making art, and playing with my cat.
